Philosophy

Body, mind and consciousness

In my therapeutic practice, body, mind and consciousness are treated as an integrated whole. Osteopathy is based on the premise that the body possesses an innate capacity for self-regulation and homeostasis -- and the therapist's role is to support these natural processes.

Each session begins with attentive listening to the patient and a thorough analysis of their condition. I do not treat symptoms -- I search for the cause.

Embryology

Cellular and tissue memory

Embryology plays a key role in my therapeutic work. The cells and tissues of our body store memories of experiences -- both physical and emotional. Through precise manual techniques, it is possible to access this memory and support the body in a process of "reprogramming."

  • Improvement of physical ailments through tissue memory work
  • Influence on emotional and psychological aspects
  • Better sleep, more energy, improved well-being
  • Restoring balance at the cellular level
Process

How a session works

Each visit begins with a detailed interview and palpatory examination. Then, using osteopathic and mikrokinesitherapeutic techniques, I work with the patient's body to find and release blockages limiting its natural function.

  • Detailed interview and health history analysis
  • Palpatory examination -- "listening" to the body
  • Gentle manual techniques tailored to individual needs
  • Personalized post-session recommendations
